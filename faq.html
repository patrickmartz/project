Can anyone train at Warrior Fit MMA?

YES! Anyone can train at Warrior Fit MMA! You don't have to be a professional or aspiring fighter. We pride ourselves on teaching any experience level from the first day beginner to those that want to compete. If you aspire to be a fighter, we can help!

Do you offer a free trial?

Absolutely. We offer a free trial week of classes of any of our classes on our regular schedule, with the exception of sparring. You are able to take multiple classes during your trial day and use the facillty during normal business hours.

How do I get a free trial? Do I need an appointment?

We do offer a free trial class during Covid. We ask that you send us an email directly at info@warriorfitMMA.com or call us at 956-655-1251 to reserve a class. We have gone to a reservation based sytem to stay safe during covid.

If you are under 18 you will need to bring a parent or guardian to sign a waiver form. We offer free trials to new clients who are local residents. If you are from outside the area, you are welcome to purchase a day pass anytime.

What do I need to bring to a Kickboxing/MMA Class?

We ask that you provide your own equipment for the kickboxing/MMA classes for general health reasons. We will cannot provide loaner gloves due to Covid restricitons. Dress in comfortable workout attire (tshirt & shorts or workout pants) and bring your own towel. Classes are taught barefoot unless you have boxing/wrestling shoes.

What do I need to bring to a Jiu-Jitsu Class?

Since we offer both GI and NO GI Jiu Jitsu classes, we ask that you dress appropriately for each class. The GI classes require a GI (with the exception of your free trial day). We do not have an official "GI dress code" and allow any belt level to wear any color GI. For NO GI classes we ask that you wear a rashguard or tight fitting shirt with MMA style shorts (no pockets, zippers or button) or compression pants.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up in person at Warrior Fit MMA or online. To sign up online, click the sign up now button and select the program and payment option you would like.
We look forward to seeing you, come in and train!! The person in the mirror is the only one stopping you!
